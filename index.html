About Us

Ghana's Trusted Name in Pure Water

Acquah Ice Natural Mineral Water is a proudly Ghanaian bottled and sachet water production company based in Mampong, Ashanti Region. We deliver clean, safe, and refreshing drinking water to homes, offices, schools, events, and businesses across the nation.

Our Mission

To produce and deliver premium quality natural mineral water that meets international standards, ensuring every Ghanaian has access to safe, pure, and healthy drinking water — anytime, anywhere.

Our Vision

To become the leading and most trusted bottled water brand in Ghana and across West Africa, recognized for our unwavering commitment to purity, quality, and customer satisfaction.
